The 4D Framework

🤝

Delegation

Determine when AI should handle tasks fully, work alongside humans, or stay out. What needs human oversight?

📝

Description

Frame problems clearly so AI understands context, scope, and constraints. Reduce ambiguity through examples.

👁️

Discernment

Recognize hallucinations, overconfidence, and edge cases. What signals indicate trustworthy output?

✅

Diligence

Maintain accountability and transparency. Who reviews, audits, and approves? What escalation triggers exist?

Learn More: The 4D Framework was developed by Dakan & Feller / Anthropic to operationalize AI fluency in real-world teams.
